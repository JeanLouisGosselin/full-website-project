Jean-Louis Gosselin


I was born in Montreal, Canada, in 1978. I started as a musician, having studied in several music schools in Canada, France and the UK.


I freelanced as a musician and conductor (having graduated from the Royal Academy of Music of London in 2004), taught in many schools as a cover teacher for Destination Education and explored the world of marketing with Boosey & Hawkes music publishers in London. All in all, I have much experience in working and collaborating with people of different backgrounds.


In the last 5 years I have developed a real passion for computer programming and web designing, having first studied C, C++, HTML, CSS and JavaScript independently, before undertaking a full-time BSc in computer science (with games technology) at City, University of London in September 2016. This passion has also led me to take on the position of freelance teacher for Geeky Kids, a programming club for children of all ages based in London.
